Account Data Protection Policy

At "Hidden Leaf Tornado", we are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring that your privacy is safeguarded. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal data that you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and game-related data.

2. Use of Data

Your data allows us to provide a personalized gaming experience, including saving game progress, customizing gameplay, and offering in-game purchases. We also use this data to communicate with you regarding updates, support issues, and promotional offers.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide you with our services, or for other essential purposes such as complying with legal obligations and resolving disputes.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data that we hold. You can also request corrections, deletions, or restrict the processing of your personal data.

If you wish to delete your account and the game data associated with it, you can send us a request, and we will take appropriate steps to fulfill it within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security that is suitable for the risks associated with the processing of personal data. This includes measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our data protection practices. We will notify you of any significant changes and encourage you to review this policy regularly.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns regarding this data protection policy, please contact us at hiddenleaftornado@gmail.com.
